§ 01 — Category

A business bridge.

Between Norway, Eastern Europe and China. The mark should carry that idea without being a literal bridge icon.

§ 02 — Audience

Industrial decision-makers.

They trust competence, not cleverness. The mark should feel institutional, but contemporary — not corporate.

§ 03 — Tone

Editorial-Nordic, not chrome-SaaS.

Paper, weight, restraint, geometry. Closer to a printed almanac than a startup brand kit.

§ 04 — Constraint

Two halves, one mark.

The name reads as "NEF" + "rød". The mark needs to live well between them, or fully replace one.
